Built for Speed and Privacy

Lightweight Speed

By stripping away unnecessary background services and UI bloat, Leef delivers a snappy, responsive experience that makes the most of your hardware.

Absolute Privacy

Your history stays on your machine. Leef collects zero telemetry: no crash reports, no usage pings, and no anonymous identifiers. What you do online is yours alone.

AI-Free Search

Leef doesn't just hide AI summaries. It stops Google AI Overviews from generating entirely, saving you bandwidth and ensuring a clean, link-first search experience. While also saving a lil bit of water!

Built-in Protection

Browse without the distractions. Leef integrates a high-performance engine that blocks ads and trackers automatically, saving you bandwidth and time.

Truly Open Source

Leef is a CC0-licensed project with a completely public codebase. No hidden binaries, no proprietary blobs. Just code you can read, audit, and trust.

Full Web Compatibility

Enjoy the engine that powers the modern web without the corporate overhead. Every site and web app works perfectly, running on your terms.

What makes Leef different?

A browser built for you,
not for advertisers.

Most modern browsers have become delivery vehicles for ads, data mining, and unwanted extras. Leef is a return to form. Built on Electron and Chromium, we provide a world-class engine without the corporate baggage.

No Bloat We removed the analytics and background processes that slow you down.
No Stealth AI Installs We don't download 4GB AI models behind your back. Leef only includes what is necessary for browsing.
Modern Foundation Built on Electron. The same tech powering VS Code, Discord, and Slack.
Curated Tools Leef ships with built-in utilities like Volume Booster and Ad-Blocker, all strictly audited by our team.
